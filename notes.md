# Course notes

## How to solve vanila problems
1. **Read and understand (2 - 3 min)**. Read the problem and understand requirements
2. **I / O (1 - 2 min).** Identify inputs and outputs
3. **Propose approach.** Propose approach and potential solution to interviewer
4. **Compare options.** If you have multiple approaches, discuss them and choose the best one
5. **Complexity.** Discuss the time and space complexity (if applicable)
6. **Implement the solution (10 - 20 min)**
7. **Test and edge cases (5 min).** Test and Write a couple of simple tests and fix edge cases.
8. **Optimize (5 min).** If have time optimize solution.
